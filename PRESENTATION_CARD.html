Problem & Solution

Problem: USIU-Africa's 78-page Student Handbook creates information overload and accessibility challenges.

Our Solution: USIU GuideBot - an intelligent Rasa-powered chatbot providing 24/7 conversational access to handbook information.

60+ Intent Categories
24/7 Availability

Methodology

Data Split: Used Rasa's DIET Classifier and TEDPolicy for intent recognition and dialogue management.

Rasa Framework DIET Classifier TEDPolicy Python NLP AWS Deployment
Deployment: Cloud-based for scalability and 24/7 availability

Background & Innovation

Educational Chatbots: Systems like Georgia Tech's Jill Watson have demonstrated AI potential in education.

Our Innovation: First comprehensive chatbot designed specifically for African university contexts, addressing unique challenges.

Key Differentiator: Context-aware responses tailored to USIU-Africa's specific policies and culture.

Data Collection & Training

Systematic Approach: Four-member team conducted comprehensive analysis of USIU-Africa Student Handbook.
